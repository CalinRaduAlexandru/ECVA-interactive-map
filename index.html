    Help us with the research

    Join the TEPACE research effort and share your perspective on Character Education in your country.

    Informational Points

    • TEPACE explores teachers’ and parents’ perspectives on Character Education across Europe.
    • Comparative mixed-methods design: questionnaires and interviews.
    • At least 11 countries involved, with 35+ researchers.

    Research Focus

    • How stakeholders perceive school-based Character Education.
    • What virtues are most valued by teachers and parents.
    • Cross-country differences in attitudes and priorities.

    Questionnaire access

    Download the questionnaire template and use it to collect local perspectives from teachers and parents.
